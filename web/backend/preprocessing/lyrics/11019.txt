8 Contributors104 Album Challenge: March 2016 LyricsThe 104 Album Challenge is a remix of the 104 book challenge. Rather than reading two books a week, Genius users plan on listening to two albums every week. Only albums released in 2016 count. There are no notability criteria, and mixtapes and EPs both count. It must have at least three tracks, or a running time of over 15 minutes.
